<h2 id="overview">Overview</h2>

The Ember is a deeply inward creative archetype. It tends to appear in people who are imaginative, flexible, introverted, and strongly caring. The defining quality is contained warmth. Instead of broadcasting energy widely, the Ember protects it, sharing depth with a small circle and developing ideas in private until they feel true.

<h2 id="strengths">Strengths</h2>

- Your inner life is rich, which gives your work and relationships unusual nuance.
- You build trust slowly, but once it exists people experience you as steady and deeply loyal.
- Solitude is productive for you rather than empty, which helps original ideas mature.
- You are often able to sense emotional undercurrents before other people name them.

<h2 id="growth-areas">Growth areas</h2>

- Protecting your energy can make it hard for others to tell when you actually want support.
- Flexible structure can slide into avoidance when a project needs firmer edges.
- You may keep editing your inner experience long after action would be more clarifying than reflection.
- Because you dislike superficiality, you can withdraw too fast from imperfect but still worthwhile connections.

<h2 id="compatible-archetypes">Compatible archetypes</h2>

The Ember usually does well with archetypes that respect privacy without demanding distance. Reliable, thoughtful partners help the Ember stay connected to the outer world without flattening its depth.
