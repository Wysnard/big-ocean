Component 1

Sealed Envelope

SVG envelope shape (inverted triangle flap + rectangle body). Pentagon seal with spin animation on tap. Transitions to portrait skeleton.

Nerin has written something for you.
The Beacon

Design Notes

  • Shape: SVG envelope — rectangle body + inverted triangle flap with visible gap between them. Filled with saturated trait colors (body: Agreeableness teal, flap: Extraversion pink).
  • Seal: Pentagon with "BO" branding. Pulses subtly (opacity 0.85→1). Spins 72° and scales up on tap before fading.
  • Breathing: Entire envelope scales 1→1.02 over 4s. Gentle, not distracting.
  • Hover: Lifts -4px, drop-shadow appears.
  • Opening: Seal spins out → envelope fades up and out → skeleton fades in (0.4s delay).
  • Skeleton: Pulsing lines representing portrait sections. Appears in the reserved space.
Component 2

Ritual Screen

Simpler background using floating geometric shapes from the identity system. Vignette overlay. Nerin speaks to both users directly.

I wrote this about the two of you.
It's better to read this together. Talk about what you're expecting before you read it.

Design Notes

  • Background: Floating geometric identity shapes (circle, half-circle, rectangle, triangle, diamond) at very low opacity (0.06→0.08). Gentle drift animation.
  • No ocean/waves: This screen uses the geometric shape identity, not the ocean layer. Clean and focused.
  • Nerin mark: 5 mini shapes in a row replacing the avatar — consistent with GeometricSignature identity system.
  • No vignette/backdrop shadows: Clean background. Geometric shapes + content are sufficient to create focus.
  • Typography: 1.6rem heading weight 600. The largest non-archetype text in the product.
  • Entrance: Shapes fade to 0.08, vignette fades in, then content rises (0.4s delay).

Design Notes

  • Phase 1 (chat): Nerin's last message visible. Natural state.
  • Phase 2 (fade, 800ms): Chat fades out. Clean canvas.
  • Phase 3 (reveal, 1000ms): Shapes + archetype name + OCEAN code appear TOGETHER. One reveal moment — the identity crystallizes.
  • Phase 4 (results, 600ms): Reveal compresses upward. Trait cards populate below with animated bars. This IS the /results page loading.
  • Total: ~2.8s. Skippable (tap viewport). Reduced motion: instant cut to results.
  • Shapes + archetype combined: Not two separate phases. The shapes ARE the archetype identity — they appear together.
