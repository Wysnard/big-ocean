Vincent Bubble — Light Mode Gradients

Dark mode is locked as Amber Depth (amber-600 → indigo #6366F1).
Below: the dark reference, then 6 light-mode gradient proposals using the Celebration palette from globals.css.

Dark Mode — Amber Depth (locked)
amber-600 → #6366F1 (dark neuroticism). This stays as-is.

Light Mode — 6 Propositions
Each gradient starts from amber and ends at a different Celebration palette color. Shown against the actual light --card: #FFF0E8 background.
Light 1
Amber Bloom
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: primary (#FF0080)

Amber into the hero pink. Mirrors the user bubble's primary→secondary energy but from the warm side. Bold and brand-forward — says "the founder is the heartbeat of this product."

Light 2
Amber Fire
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: secondary (#FF6B2B)

Pure warm spectrum — amber melting into secondary orange. The most cohesive with the Celebration theme's warm tones. Feels natural against the cream card. Low risk, high warmth.

Light 3
Amber Reef
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: tertiary (#00B4A6)

Warm-to-cool crossfade — the most visually distinctive option. The teal endpoint is the light-mode tertiary, so it echoes Nerin's avatar palette without being the same. Creates the "sunlight hitting the ocean" metaphor.

Light 4
Amber Cosmos
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: openness (#A855F7)

Amber dissolving into the openness purple. The closest spiritual cousin to the dark-mode Amber Depth — both gradients end in a purple/indigo family, creating a sense of "same identity, different mood" across themes.

Light 5
Amber Warmth
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: amber-800 (#92400e)

Stays entirely in the amber lane — golden to burnt sienna. The safest, most "human" option. Strong contrast against the cream background. Doesn't borrow from any trait or brand color so it can't clash with anything.

Light 6
Amber Flare
from: amber-500 (#f59e0b) → to: dark-tertiary (#FF2D9B)

Amber into the dark-mode tertiary hot pink — which in light mode becomes a playful, energetic accent. Brighter and more electric than Amber Bloom. Feels festive and "Celebration"-themed. The most fun option.
