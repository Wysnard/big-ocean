Design Notes

  • Gradient border shifts from Pink→Teal (light) and Teal→Gold (dark)
  • Floating glow orbs with subtle animation add atmosphere without distraction
  • Celebration gradient CTA for sign-up; single-color for sign-in (differentiated energy)
  • Focus states use primary color ring with soft glow
  • Warm Cream / Deep Navy backgrounds with blush / midnight inputs

Direction 2: Split Accent

A bold vertical rainbow bar anchors the left edge. Clean, editorial layout. Tags communicate context. The boldness is structural, not decorative.

Design Notes

  • Vertical gradient bar (6px) is the only bold element — all brand energy concentrated in one line
  • Tag pill at the top sets context with primary tint
  • White / Deep Navy card backgrounds for maximum contrast
  • "or" divider creates clear visual separation
  • Most restrained of the 4 directions — editorial, clean, professional

Design Notes

  • OCEAN shapes row (circle, half-circle, rectangle, triangle, diamond) connects auth to the personality framework
  • Gradient text accent on key word in heading (Pink→Orange / Teal→Gold)
  • Dark button with brand reveal on hover — neutral at rest, Electric Pink on hover (light) / Teal on hover (dark)
  • Corner shape decorations at low opacity add depth without clutter
  • Most branded of the 4 directions — OCEAN identity visible throughout

Direction 4: Floating Card

Elevated, airy, warm. The brand mark anchors identity at the top. Borderless inputs feel modern. A subtle wave accent at the bottom hints at the ocean. Ghost buttons for secondary actions.

Design Notes

  • big-[shapes] brand mark anchors identity at the top of the form

  • Wave SVG accent at bottom is barely visible (4% opacity) — subtle ocean reference
  • Ghost button for secondary action (sign in / sign up toggle) is clean and uncluttered
  • Most balanced of the 4 directions — warm, branded, approachable
