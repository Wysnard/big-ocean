Meet Nerin

The guide reimagined through Japanese kamon — family crests born from the sea. Five ocean currents, five dimensions of self. Centuries of visual discipline meets the depths of personality.

Sea Kamon · Round 8
Concept 01
Itsutsu Nami
Five Waves Mon · Pinwheel Current

Five stylized wave crests — each a Big Five trait — arranged in a circular pinwheel inside the traditional mon ring. Each wave curls inward, creating a rotational flow. The center holds a small circle: the calm eye where all currents meet. A kamon of converging ocean forces.

Conveys: Flowing convergence, five forces meeting at the center of self
Concept 02
Uzumaki
Whirlpool Mon · Five Spiral Arms

A whirlpool kamon — five spiral arms unfurling from a single center point, each a different trait color. The arms don't compete; they create one unified rotational motion. The whirlpool draws you inward, toward self-knowledge. Nerin as the vortex that reveals depth.

Conveys: Depth drawing inward, self-discovery as a current
Concept 03
Kai-awase
Nautilus Mon · Chambered Spiral

A nautilus shell rendered as kamon — each chamber a different trait color spiraling inward. The logarithmic spiral is nature's own golden ratio, each chamber building on the last. Five chambers, five traits, one continuous curve. Personality as a structure that grows from its own center. The spiral leads inward to the core of who you are.

Conveys: Natural growth, layered self-knowledge, golden proportion
Concept 04
Seigaiha
Wave Arcs Mon · Compass Rose

The traditional seigaiha (blue sea wave) pattern reimagined as a compass rose. Concentric arcs radiate outward in five sections — each section a trait color, each arc fading from solid to ghost. A small directional arrow at the top breaks the symmetry. The repeating wave pattern is order; the five colors are personality. Ancient Japanese ocean motif meets directional guidance.

Conveys: Ordered rhythm, ocean tradition, directional clarity
Concept 05
Kurage
Jellyfish Mon · Trailing Dimensions

A jellyfish kamon — the bell is a layered dome of translucent brand color, and five tentacles trail downward, each a Big Five trait. The jellyfish drifts through the ocean guided by currents, not fighting them. Nerin as a creature of graceful navigation, illuminated from within. The tentacles are the five dimensions reaching into the depths of personality.

Conveys: Graceful navigation, illuminated depth, effortless guidance
Concept 06
Torii Nami
Gate over Water · Threshold Mon

A torii gate standing in water — the iconic image of Itsukushima reimagined as kamon. The gate is the threshold between surface and depth, known and unknown. Beneath it, five horizontal wave lines layer in trait colors, each receding into transparency. A small light glows at the gate's center. Nerin as the passage into self-understanding, standing calm above the ocean of personality.

Conveys: Sacred threshold, passage to self-knowledge, calm authority
