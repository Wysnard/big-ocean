PROPOSITION B
Mini Ring (Recommended)

A tiny circular progress ring echoing the ConfidenceRingCard. Creates visual system coherence — the ring metaphor ("how filled in is our picture") maps naturally to confidence. Low confidence = partial ring + muted color.

PROPOSITION C
Integrated Score Bar

Confidence is communicated through the score bar itself — full opacity for high confidence, translucent + diagonal stripes for low confidence. A subtle label below the bar spells it out. The card stays visually clean.
